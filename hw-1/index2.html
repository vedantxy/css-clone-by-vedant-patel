What is Nansen AI?
Nansen AI is the start of a new way to trade. Instead of spending hours jumping between dashboards, just ask about anything onchain - smart money movements, token flows, or market trends - through our mobile app. This agentic interface allows any investors and traders to manage their portfolio, get real-time onchain insights, and stack Points, directly from your phone.
Is this just a mobile version of the web app?
They are complementary, but instead of clicking through dashboards and charts, you now have the ability to have a natural voice conversation about any onchain question and get the exact insights you need. It's research at the speed of thought, optimized for mobile.
